Background

The client is a BSIT student at Bukidnon State University. Finding boarding houses near the university was extremely challenging and exhausting. The process required physically walking from one location to another in the heat, making it time-consuming and draining. Key factors for comfortable accommodation include price, location, rules, wifi, house policies, and basic etiquette. Comparing different options was difficult due to incomplete details and inaccurate information in online listings.

"Our experience in looking for a boarding house near the university was really challenging jud. Kapoy kaayo siya kay maglakaw-lakaw mi from one boarding house to another nya init pajud kayo. Time consuming kaayo and draining sad physically."

— Jely Bojo, 1st Year BSIT Student

Phone-Based Booking

Cash Payments

Paper Contracts

Rental agreements stored in filing cabinets, prone to damage and loss

No Verification

No systematic background checking or landlord accreditation process

System Overview

Haven Space is a Web-Based and Mobile Boarding House Management System that centralizes all boarding house operations into a single platform, connecting the administration office, landlords, and student boarders through role-based dashboards.

Boarders

Browse listings, book rooms, make secure payments, and communicate with landlords

Landlords

Manage properties, screen tenants, track bookings, and monitor income

Admin

Oversee platform operations, manage users, and generate reports

System Features

User Features (Boarders & Landlords)

  • User registration and login with role selection
  • Property listing browsing with search and filters
  • Interactive map view for location-based search
  • Online booking and reservation system
  • In-platform messaging and notifications

  • Booking history and favorites management
  • Profile management and document upload

Admin Features

  • User management (approve, suspend, verify)

  • Booking oversight and dispute resolution
